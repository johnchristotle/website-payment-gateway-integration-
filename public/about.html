John Christotle

ABOUT

Christotle dedicated time in his youthful years to his local church playing, singing in the choir from age 12. His music profession started in 2004 and in 2007 he signed a record deal with GlobDisk Music in respect to his first album "She's Your Wife". Not too long, he left the secular music line and switched to Gospel where he now stood on his own and formed a band under John Christotle Entertainment; playing in various occasions and organizing street to church music concerts.

In 2010 he released a debut gospel album title "Carry Me Jeje" under MBASON MUSIC which led him to limelight. He display the steps, the style, the passion, the anointing and fire whenever he minister on stage and his fans call him ''The Papa-yo Master''

He has written several stage plays, songs, poems and numerous articles.

Songs to his credit include:

  • The Heroes Anthem
  • Daddy King Jingle (DBS Warri)
  • Wazobia Fm Jingle (PH)
  • The Rivers State 2010 Peace Concert Theme Song
  • Close-Up Loves Naija Song Remix.
  • Ifeanyi Okowa's 2015 Governorship campain Jingle
